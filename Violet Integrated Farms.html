Ginger

One of our product is Ginger, an underground rhizome of a perennial crop called ginger (zingiberofficinale). We process the ginger into saleable forms such as dried, powdered, and packaged as a beverage (ginger tea) for consumers. Ginger is an important perennial crop with versatile applications.

    The Process Of Ginger Cultivation

    We clear the area of bushes or stubbles of previous crop to facilitate land preparation. Plowing takes place twice before harrow to pulverize the soil and compost with chicken manure. Planting; this is done at the start of the rainy season (usually April - May). Land fertilization; usually a hectare require 11.5 bags 14-14-14 and 4 bags 0-0-60 in addition to 5t=hectare chicken or animal manure. Weeding, Irrigation. Harvesting; normally takes place about 8-10 months after planting depending on the variety and market requirements. Post Harvest Handling; cleaning & washing. Curing under 90% relative humidity and for a required period. Sorting & classification accordingly. Storage / Marketing.
